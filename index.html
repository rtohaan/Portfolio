Hey! I'm Rohan

Fascinated by wonders of nature, I chose Computer Science as my field to help me fulfill my curiousity for Space/Physics and search for life on beyond our planet. I completed my undergraduate studies at CUNY Hunter College in 2020. At the moment, I am deepy passionate about building sites and how they interact with humans and parts of the internet. You can checkout some of my work here.

In my free time, you can find me practicing on a piano, lifting weights and wondering what's beyond Earth. I also love learning about Cryptocurrency and Blockchain as I believe blockchain has the potential to revolutionize our world.

Work

You can view all of my work in my github.

Blitz

A web application which allows a group of users to split their expenses in a efficient and collaborative manner. I worked a front-end engineer on this project where I leveraged Material-UI and used multiple sites to provide the best user-friendly experience which can be scaled across multile devices.

Alexa Memes Skill

Worked on an Alexa Skill at my first hackathon, at HackNY, where also we won the best domain name. This skill will send a meme from a subreddit as a text message to our phone.

NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day App

A program that allows you to download nasa's picture using their API and automatically set the picture as your desktop's wallpaper.

I've also deployed a discord bot which provides the picture in my server.
